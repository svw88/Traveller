Each and every one of us has asked ourselves the question at some point in our lives "what is there to do today?".    The answer to that question for most of us is usually found after tireless searching on cluttered websites and endless messages to friends.  However we at The Traveller Company believe that we have created a way to turn that tireless searching into a simple two click process that is both elegant and fun to use.

Traveller-events is a web based application that was designed to create a space for businesses and promoters to post their events and provide simple easy access for the public to those events.
